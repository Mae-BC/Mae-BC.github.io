Growth Mindset: Discuss what it is and why it is relevant.

"A growth mindset, as conceived by Stanford psychologist Carol Dweck and colleagues, is the belief that a person's capacities and talents can be improved over time." The growth mindset is goes hand in hand with neuroplasticity. You can understand everything there is to know about neuroplasticity and how it effects you and your brain but without a growth mindset that is worth nothing. A growth mindset reminds us that we always have room to grow and learn. We need that if we want to improve in any area of our lives.
